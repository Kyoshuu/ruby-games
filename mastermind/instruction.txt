      Welcome to MASTERMIND!
Your mission is to guess what code
game has generated. You have 12 chances
to do it.

Board Explanation:
p => numbers in good position
c => good numbers(not in good position)

Controls:
[1-4] [1-6] - First part is for selecting
    position, second part to select number
    you want guess ex. "1 6"
confirm - Confirms your choice
exit - Ends game
